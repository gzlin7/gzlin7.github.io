Chinese Calligraphy

This past January, I took a Chinese calligraphy class taught by Teacher Zhou 周老师. The class met every day for three hours, and most students, like myself, had no prior experience doing Chinese calligraphy. 周老师 taught us not only calligraphy strokes and techniques, but also the history, philosophy, and evolution of Chinese calligraphy.

The class coincided with Chinese New Year, so I was able to make many fortune 福 signs (like the one in the video!) to give to my friends and floormates, wishing them luck for the new year. I also made spring couplets 春联 to hang on my dorm door. The class culminated in a video project where my friend and I explored Boston Chinatown, identifying different historical styles of calligraphy on signs, packaging, and landmarks, which we definitely would not have noticed before. 谢谢周老师!
